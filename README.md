# Leave Management Automation with Google Workspace

This project automates the entire leave request and approval process using **Google Workspace tools**. It streamlines HR operations by eliminating manual paperwork, providing automated email notifications, and maintaining comprehensive leave records with approval workflows.

## Technologies Used
- **Google Apps Script** – Backend automation
- **Google Forms** – Leave request submissions
- **Google Sheets** – Leave tracking and data storage
- **Google Calendar** – Visualizing approved leaves
- **Email Notifications** – Automated updates for employees and approvers

## Key Features
- **Automated Leave Request Submissions:** Employees can submit leave requests via Google Forms.
- **Multi-Level Approval Workflows:** Approvals routed automatically to designated managers or HR personnel.
- **Automated Email Notifications:** Notifications sent to all stakeholders when leave is requested, approved, or rejected.
- **Real-Time Leave Balance Tracking:** Keeps employees and HR informed of remaining leave balances.
- **Calendar Integration:** Approved leaves are automatically added to Google Calendar for easy visualization.
- **Mobile-Accessible:** Employees can submit and track leave requests from any device.

## Getting Started
1. **Set up the Google Form** for leave requests.
2. **Link the Form to a Google Sheet** to capture responses.
3. **Deploy the Google Apps Script** to automate approval workflows and notifications.
4. **Integrate with Google Calendar** to visualize approved leaves.
5. **Customize email templates** as needed for notifications.

## Benefits
- Reduces manual HR workload
- Minimizes errors in leave tracking
- Speeds up the approval process
- Provides transparency and accessibility for all employees
